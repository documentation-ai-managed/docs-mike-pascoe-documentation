## Creating Your Account

Getting started with Linktree is simple. Follow these steps to create your first link page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user">
    Visit linktree.com and click "Sign up for free." Enter your email and create a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Profile" icon="settings">
    Add your profile picture, bio, and display name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Links" icon="plus">
    Click "Add Link" to include your social media, website, and other important URLs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

<Callout kind="alert">If links aren't working, check your URLs for typos.</Callout>

Common problems and solutions:

- **Links not clickable**: Ensure URLs start with `https://`
- **Page not loading**: Clear your browser cache
- **Changes not saving**: Check your internet connection
